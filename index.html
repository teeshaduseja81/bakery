ABOUT US

With roots dating back to 2003, Cinnamint Bakery embodies the seamless fusion of classic flavors and inventive culinary approaches. Upholding an unwavering commitment to excellence, our skilled team, led by Mr. Ken McMeikan, crafts each treat with enchanting expertise. From the iconic Cinnamint cakes to contemporary reinterpretations of beloved recipes, every bite narrates our devotion to the baking craft. Embark on a journey of taste, where tradition and innovation intertwine seamlessly at Cinnamint Bakery.
